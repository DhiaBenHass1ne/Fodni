F.A.Q.

Fodni is an online platform that connects service providers with clients, creating a marketplace for freelancers and businesses to collaborate on various projects.

Clients can sign up, post their project details, and receive proposals from skilled freelancers. They can review profiles, chat with freelancers, and choose the best fit for their needs.

Freelancers can create a profile showcasing their skills and expertise. Once verified, they can browse available projects, submit proposals, and collaborate with clients directly through our platform.

We prioritize quality by implementing a review and rating system. Clients can leave feedback on freelancers, and freelancers can rate their experience with clients. This helps build a trustworthy and reliable community.
